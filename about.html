How RED Began

RED Studio was founded in 2024 in Karnataka by Chris Zeyon Pinto with a simple but powerful belief — every story deserves to be told through light, motion, and emotion.

What started as a photography and videography studio quickly evolved into something bigger. Today, RED is a full-spectrum creative house that captures moments through the lens and builds unforgettable digital experiences through code.

About RED

RED Studio is more than a creative agency.
We are storytellers, light painters, and digital architects.

We specialize in cinematic photography, high-end videography, short-form content, and now premium website development under our CODE RED division.

Every project — whether a single frame or an entire digital ecosystem — is crafted with obsessive attention to emotion, contrast, and detail. Our work doesn’t just look good. It feels alive.
